Cabio design directions

Three standalone UI proposals for the app shell, graph workspace, controls, config bar, and recording/crossover surfaces.

Exploration only

1. Benchline

Dark, quiet, technical. Thin rails, clear measurement focus, little decoration. Best if Cabio should feel like precise lab software.

Strength: very focused, low visual noise. Risk: can feel a little austere.

Light, spacious, product-design feeling. The graph remains central, while measurement and setup read like an inspector panel.

Strength: approachable and polished. Risk: less dense for power users.

3. Matrix Console

Dense, modular, console-like. Strongest for advanced users who want all major state visible without scrolling or visual softness.

Strength: compact and distinctive. Risk: can feel more intense.
